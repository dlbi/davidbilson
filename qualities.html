7 Top Qualities That Make A Successful Developer





Writer: David Bilson



If you really want to be a great developer, there are some key qualities you must have.


Although there are plenty jobs out there, we cannot overlook the fact that the competition is fierce.

A budding software developer still has to be at the top of their game and stand out from the crowd in order to get their dream job.

According to the CEO of CODEFIGHTS, Tigran Sloyan, there are seven important qualities that all successful developers must have.


Be a team player

Developers who are team players are more likely to be successful in their career than lone-rangers. There is a pop culture stereotype of the brilliant but emotionally stunted programmer - someone who can pu; of fears of code heroically but can't connect with the people around them.

This doesn't work in real engineering teams. As a developer, your job is to solve issues and create a great product, not just to write as many clever lines of codes as possible. That means communication with all kinds of people such as other developers, quality assurance, PMs, sales, customers etc is of high essence.

Remeber to treat others with sympathy and respect. Be the kind of developoer who works well and lifts up everyone around you, not just the one that works well in isolation.


Stay humble

Developers that are successful never forget to drop their egos at home. No matter how good you are at what you do, you are not always going to be right. Other team members might have constructive criticism during code review, or your code might have bugs that you onli find out about after shipping the product.

Whenever this happens, you have to be OKAY with other people pointing out and correcting your errors. That is why humility is such an important quality for developers to have.

The ability to be humble and take criticism and corrections from others and then channeling it into learning what you can do beter will surely make you a much better developer in the long run.


Practice makes perfect

Successful developers keeps their skills sharp. You have to put in the time and effort to sharpen your skills.

Outstanding developers are much like outstanding artists. What they are creating is not just work they do to collect a cheque. They always have a sense of craftmanship and authorship.
